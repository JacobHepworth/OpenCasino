♠ Blackjack

Beat the dealer by getting closer to 21 without going over.

Card Values
  • Number cards = face value
  • J, Q, K = 10
  • Ace = 1 or 11
Actions
  • Hit — draw another card
  • Stand — keep your hand
  • Double — double your bet, take one card
  • Split — split a pair into two hands
Payouts
  • Win → 1:1
  • Blackjack (A + 10) → 3:2
  • Dealer busts → you win

🎡 Roulette

Bet on where the ball will land on the spinning wheel (0–36).

Bet Types
  • Straight — single number → 35:1
  • Red/Black — color → 1:1
  • Odd/Even → 1:1
  • Low (1–18) / High (19–36) → 1:1
  • Dozens (1–12, 13–24, 25–36) → 2:1
  • Columns → 2:1

Landing on 0 wins only straight bets on 0.

🎴 Baccarat

Bet on which hand wins — Player, Banker, or a Tie. The hand closest to 9 wins.

Card Values
  • Ace = 1
  • 2–9 = face value
  • 10, J, Q, K = 0
  • Scores wrap around mod 10 (e.g. 15 → 5)
Drawing Rules
  • 8 or 9 on first two cards = Natural — no more cards drawn
  • Player draws a third card if score is 0–5
  • Banker draws based on their score and Player's third card
Payouts
  • Player wins → 1:1
  • Banker wins → 0.95:1 (5% commission)
  • Tie → 8:1 (push on Player/Banker bets)

You can only bet on one outcome per round. Click a zone to place your chip.
